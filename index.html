About Me.

As a biomedical engineer, my journey has been deeply intertwined with harnessing technology for healthcare. I've had the privilege of working on cognitive video games tailored towards healthcare solutions. These projects not only combine my expertise but also reflect a personal mission to transform therapeutic interventions in imaginative ways.

Diving further into immersive experiences, I've ventured into developing VR applications, bringing a novel dimension to patient care. Biomedical data processing is another area close to my heart, where I apply meticulous analysis for diagnostics and brain-computer interfaces. Beyond my technical pursuits, art and design have always been sources of inspiration, influencing my work's aesthetic and functionality. Continuous learning, for me, is both a professional commitment and a personal joy.
